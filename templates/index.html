What We Solve

Every pain point, addressed.

📋

Live Menu at Every Table

Customers browse the full menu with prices, photos, and real-time availability — no guessing, no waiting for a waiter.

⏱️

First Come, First Served

Orders enter a strict time-based queue. No more late customers jumping ahead — every table gets fair treatment.

📡

Real-time Order Tracking

Customers watch their order move from Placed → Preparing → Ready live on their table screen. No anxiety, no confusion.

🤖

AI Food Assistant

Ask the chatbot anything — "What's spicy?", "Best dish today?" — and get instant smart recommendations powered by AI.

🔔

Call Waiter Button

Not comfortable with screens? One tap calls a waiter instantly. They can even place the order on the customer's behalf.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Admin, Kitchen, Manager and Customer — each person sees only what they need. Secure, bcrypt-protected login for every role.
